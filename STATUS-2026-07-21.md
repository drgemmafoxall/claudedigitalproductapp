### What's working right now
- **Core generation loop** — type/paste notes, upload a doc, or record/upload audio → AI turns it into a structured product in your brand voice, using your exact phrasing wherever possible, with `[NEEDS GEMMA]` gap markers for anything it can't invent.
- **21 product types**, one shared registry: checklists, tip sheets, explainers, infographics, one-pagers, workbooks, resource guides, scripts, guide/toolkits, planners/trackers, lead magnet bundles, e-books, 4 social post types, mini-courses, Vizard clip briefs, Suno audio kits, and now **two** MagicLight video kits.
- **Brand system enforced in code, not just guidelines** — colours, fonts, the contrast law (illegal colour combos like white-on-sage are structurally impossible), fixed footer/disclaimer/copyright, audience registers.
- **Multi-audience selection** — pick one, several, or "General / all audiences," plus a new **"Children (developmental age 3–12)"** audience with genuinely simplified, literal language rules.
- **PDF rendering** — branded A4 documents and full e-books, with embedded fonts (no external font-fetch dependency), and automatic shrink-to-fit for one-page product types that slightly overflow.
- **Image generation** — Gemini "Nano Banana" illustrations, brand-locked (no real people/photos, no in-image text, fixed palette), generated per product from the review screen.
- **Google Drive export** — both generated images and exported PDFs can push straight into your Drive folders via a service account (no manual download/upload).
- **Library** — every generated product now saves to Supabase automatically the moment it's created, and the Library page lists everything with one-click PDF re-download.
- **Holiday Club character cast** — Penny, Max, Aria, Tina, Toby, Will, and Miss Clara, fully documented with "show not tell" story rules baked into generation, plus a Dr Gemma cartoon avatar for adult explainers. First-pass concept sketches sent separately.
- **Content360 CSV export** — matches your real bulk-upload template exactly (columns verified against your actual spreadsheet).
- **Course pack export** — mini-courses export as a ZIP (lessons, quizzes, workbook spec, opt-in copy) ready for manual Systeme.io import.
